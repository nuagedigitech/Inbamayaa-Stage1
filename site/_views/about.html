About Inbamayaa Foundation

Lets help them
together.

Inbamayaa Foundation has been launched since July 2016 by Ranjithha Gunnia and has a headquarter out of Madurai. We have been always willing to do something big for Underprivileged Students Education Support & For old age home people Medical Treatment help & for providing food and clothes to the poor needles & for spreading environmental awareness to people etc.,
‍
Our Inbamayaa Foundation inspired by the great Kamarajar & APJ Abdul Kalam to support one lac government school students within 2020 for providing educational materials & for motivating one lac talented students by distributing certificates & awarded medals by all over districts around Tamilnadu to facilitate them in developing their study.
Expecting each one of contribution would be an added value to support our 2020 dream project kalam vision to provide certificates,books & awarded medals for motivating 20 talented students in each government school and to reach the target as one lac govt school talented students by supporting educational materials for the poor needy students all over Tamilnadu!
‍
Inbamayaa Foundation always want to motivate the students to get the best education for their future, no matter what situation that they have to go through. Therefore, besides those facilitations, we also support the children who came from poorer families in their educational expenses.

Education
Support Children's Education

The education of poor and needy children is one of the top priorities at the Inbamayaa Foundation. We place the utmost importance on helping support the children in their educational pursuits and encourage them to excel at their studies and extracurricular activities. We advice slum children to go schools and colleges based on their prior education. This allows the poor and the farming children to have the opportunity to gain a proper education.
Apart from supporting children, we also support the educational expertise of children from poorer families in the surrounding areas of Madurai. We provide them with free school books and uniforms, We give them school textbooks, notebooks, learning material and kits to aid them in their learning development. We also encourage them to pursue creative skills, sports and games for better all-round growth

Feeding The Poor
Providing Quality Meals

Our attempt is to feed the millions of children in India who have the zeal to learn and achieve, but not the means. By feeding them a day, we give them the motivation and nourishment they need to pursue an education and a better future. It is our endeavor to reach out to every child at the grass root level of the society,A majority of the population in India is still unable to get at least one complete square meal a day. Inbamayaa has resolved to liberate the underprivileged children from the slum and rural areas of poverty and illiteracy by serving them. We aim to provide the children and roadside platform people with quality meals to satisfy the poor neediest.

Environmental Awareness
Ecosystem And Pollution Awareness

We educate the poor children about the environment and social ecosystems We organised various educational awareness programmes on environmental issues like pollution, ecosystem balance in the society by developing educational teaching materials and aids for making children easily understand environmental issues. We also seek the support of the government media and other organisations to support our endeavors. This raises people's awareness for the preservation and conservation of the environment. We encourage the children and elders to learn how to garden and take care of the plants and trees.

Medical Camps
Medical Camps

Inbamayaa conducting health campaign by taking care of old age people residing in village areas to provide free checkup and distributing organic food monthly once. Health is always wealth!

Clothes Distribution
Donating Clothes To The Neediest

While donating clothes is deeply satisfying. By letting donors actually see the recipients' via Facebook, Inbamayaa solves the problem of transparency with joyful simplicity  giving many more donors the confidence to come forward and support the cause, and paving the way for nothing short of a social revolution.
